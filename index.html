About the Museum

The Football Museum celebrates the global sport through immersive exhibits, original memorabilia, and interactive displays. From World Cup triumphs to grassroots stories, our museum brings fans closer to the soul of football.

Plan your visit

Located in the heart of the city, the Football Museum is open seven days a week. Whether you're a lifelong fan or a curious newcomer, there's something here for everyone. Book tickets online or visit our gift shop for unique football collectibles.
